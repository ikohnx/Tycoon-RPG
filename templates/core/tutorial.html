{% if section.id == 'welcome' %}

Welcome to Business Tycoon RPG! This game teaches you real-world business skills through interactive scenarios and challenges.

Your goal is to build your business empire by mastering six core disciplines:

  • Marketing - Learn to attract and retain customers
  • Finance & Accounting - Master budgeting, bookkeeping, financial statements, and investment analysis
  • Operations - Optimize processes and supply chains
  • Human Resources - Build and manage winning teams
  • Legal - Navigate contracts and compliance
  • Strategy - Make decisions that shape your business future
Tip: Start with scenarios in your chosen industry to build familiarity, then branch out to other disciplines as you level up!
{% elif section.id == 'navigation' %}

The Hub is your command center. From here, you can access all game features:

  • Scenarios - Story-driven business challenges with choices and consequences
  • Challenges - Interactive exercises like budgeting, ROI calculations, and break-even analysis
  • Training - Learn concepts before applying them in gameplay
  • Map - Visual campaign progress across different worlds
  • Shop - Purchase equipment and items to boost your abilities
  • Profile - View your stats, achievements, and progress
Note: The top navigation bar shows your current cash, energy, and quick access to key features. Energy regenerates over time or can be restored with items.
{% elif section.id == 'scenarios' %}

Scenarios are the heart of the game. Each scenario presents a business situation where you must make decisions.

Here's how scenarios work:

  • Read the situation - Understand the business context and challenge
  • Consider your options - Each choice has different outcomes
  • Make your decision - Your choice affects cash, reputation, and experience
  • Learn from feedback - See why certain choices are better than others

Scenarios are rated with stars (1-3) based on how well you perform. Better choices earn more stars, XP, and rewards!

Tip: Don't worry about making mistakes - they're learning opportunities! You can replay scenarios to improve your score.
{% elif section.id == 'disciplines' %}

Master all six business disciplines to become a true Business Tycoon:

  • Marketing (Level 1-10) - Customer acquisition, branding, advertising, market research, and promotion strategies
  • Finance & Accounting (Level 1-10) - The accounting equation, journal entries, budgeting, cash flow, financial statements, investments, and risk management
  • Operations (Level 1-10) - Supply chain, inventory, quality control, and process optimization
  • Human Resources (Level 1-10) - Hiring, training, performance management, and team building
  • Legal (Level 1-10) - Contracts, compliance, intellectual property, and business law
  • Strategy (Level 1-10) - Business planning, competitive analysis, and long-term decision making
Progression: Each discipline has 10 levels. Complete scenarios and challenges in that discipline to earn XP and level up. Higher levels unlock new content and abilities!
{% elif section.id == 'challenges' %}

Interactive Challenges test your skills with hands-on exercises:

  • Budget Calculator - Create and balance business budgets
  • ROI Analysis - Calculate return on investment for different options
  • Break-Even Analysis - Determine when a business becomes profitable
  • Cash Flow Forecasting - Project future cash positions
  • Negotiation Simulator - Practice deal-making and bargaining
  • Risk Assessment - Identify and mitigate business risks

Challenges provide immediate feedback on your calculations and decisions, helping you learn practical business skills.

Tip: Use the Training section first to learn the formulas and concepts, then apply them in challenges!
{% elif section.id == 'simulations' %}

Advanced Simulations let you run complex business scenarios over time:

  • Market Simulation - Set prices, respond to competition, and maximize profit
  • Supply Chain Simulator - Manage inventory, suppliers, and logistics
  • HR Management - Hire, develop, and retain employees
  • Investor Pitch - Create pitch decks and present to virtual investors
  • Business Plan Workshop - Develop comprehensive business plans

Simulations run over multiple "weeks" or "months" in-game, letting you see the long-term impact of your decisions.

Unlock Requirement: Most advanced simulations require reaching higher levels in relevant disciplines. Keep progressing to unlock new content!
